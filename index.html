Partnering with Girls Who Code

Helping to diversify code.

Our local chapter is teaming up with Girls Who Code as part of a larger mission to bring diversity to the field of computer science and give more opportunities to aspiring students. This is being realized at schools across Columbia, MO with regular sessions that enlighten students on the many applications of the field while also providing support in learning the many elements of software development.
